WHO AM I?

I'm Jakub Witucki aka ROKEFELA, a producer based in Poland, born ‘93. Known for producing “Be You” by VanVan and Heiress Harris (T.I.’s and Tiny’s Daughter) which was performed at BET Awards 2024.

Raised in the nineties, I was steeped in its varied music. My father and brother took care to teach what good taste was, regardless of genre. At 17, I started producing beats, experiencing and joining the local scene.
So far done a lot of engineering work, recording, mixing, mastering and songwriting, to better support local and international upcoming artists.

Hip-hop was always an important thing in my life. Influenced by many legends who continue to inspire me, including: Timbaland, DJ Premier, Zaytoven, London On Da Track, Tay Keith, D.A. Got That Dope, Go Grizzly, Bandplay and many others.
